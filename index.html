Research

My research interests are interdicplinary including device physics, analog and RF circuit design, and machine learning. My main focus is on circuit and system design for high-speed and RF applications used in wireless transmission and sensing. Currently, I am working on my honors research thesis that combines RF circuit design with aspects of machine learning and optimization. The goal is to leverage machine learning to assist with the VCO design flow.
